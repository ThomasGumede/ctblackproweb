Established in Cape Town in 2010, CT Black Pro Golf Club is more than a sporting institution—it's a vibrant community dedicated to excellence, empowerment, and transformation through golf.

We provide a dynamic platform for Black golfers to thrive, connect, and lead, while fostering a culture of pride, professionalism, and purpose on and off the course.

  • Inclusive Environment
  • Professional Development
  • Community Impact
  • Legacy Building

To advance Black excellence in golf by creating inclusive opportunities, nurturing talent, and building a legacy of pride, professionalism, and community leadership.
