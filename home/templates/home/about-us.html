About Us

Founded in Cape Town in 2010, CT Black Pro Golf Club stands as a proud symbol of excellence, inclusion, and community in South African golf. Dedicated to nurturing talent and celebrating diversity, the club offers a welcoming space for golfers of all backgrounds to refine their skills, connect through sport, and champion the spirit of unity on the fairway. With a commitment to professionalism and empowerment, CT Black Pro continues to shape the future of golf—one swing at a time.

We provide a dynamic platform for Black golfers to thrive, connect, and lead, while fostering a culture of pride, professionalism, and purpose on and off the course.

Vision And Mission

To advance Black excellence in golf by creating inclusive opportunities, nurturing talent, and building a legacy of pride, professionalism, and community leadership.

Why Choose Us?

At CT Black Pro Golf Club, we believe golf is more than a sport—it's a platform for transformation. Our club offers a unique blend of professional growth, cultural pride, and community connection, tailored for those who aspire to lead both on and off the course. With expert mentorship, inclusive values, and a legacy-driven spirit, we're not just shaping golfers—we're shaping changemakers.
