CT Black Pro Golf Club

The golf club was established in
Cape Town, Western Cape, RSA, in 2010

Founded in Cape Town in 2010, CT Black Pro Golf Club stands as a proud symbol of excellence, inclusion, and community in South African golf. Dedicated to nurturing talent and celebrating diversity, the club offers a welcoming space for golfers of all backgrounds to refine their skills, connect through sport, and champion the spirit of unity on the fairway. With a commitment to professionalism and empowerment, CT Black Pro continues to shape the future of golf—one swing at a time.
