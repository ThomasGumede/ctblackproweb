about us

The golf club was established in Cape Town, Western Cape, RSA, in 2010

Founded in Cape Town in 2010, CT Black Pro Golf Club stands as a proud symbol of excellence, inclusion, and community in South African golf. Dedicated to nurturing talent and celebrating diversity, the club offers a welcoming space for golfers of all backgrounds to refine their skills, connect through sport, and champion the spirit of unity on the fairway. With a commitment to professionalism and empowerment, CT Black Pro continues to shape the future of golf—one swing at a time.

Why Join CT Black Pro Golf Club?

More Than a Club—A Community of Champions.

At CT Black Pro Golf Club, we don't just play golf—we build legacy. Since 2010, we've been committed to creating an inclusive, empowering space where excellence thrives and every swing tells a story. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, our club offers mentorship, camaraderie, and a shared passion for the game. Join us, and be part of a movement that's reshaping golf in Cape Town and beyond.
